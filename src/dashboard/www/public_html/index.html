Submissions to the Research Excellence Framework (REF) were made into 34 units of assessment divided into four Main Panels:

Main Panel A: Medicine, Health, and Life Sciences
Main Panel B: Physical Sciences, Engineering, and Mathematics
Main Panel C: Social Sciences
Main Panel D: Arts and Humanities

The results shown here are limited to SHAPE disciplines (Social Sciences, Humanities and the Arts for People and the Economy). This includes Main Panels C and D as well as unit of assessment 4 from Main Panel A (Psychology, Psychiatry, and Neuroscience).
